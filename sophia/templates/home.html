Comfortable Environment

We strive to provide a welcoming space for students to learn at their own pace. Violin lessons are conducted out of our home in Arlington Heights, IL. We are minutes away from Schaumburg, Mount Prospect, and Elk Grove Village.

Suzuki Method

We employ the Suzuki method of teaching in our violin lessons, which emphasizes hands on learning, comfort with the instrument, and parental involvement with children. From child to adult, we believe anyone can learn the violin. Whether you're a beginner or more advanced, our violin lessons are catered towards achieving your musical goals.

Free Trial Violin Lesson

We offer a free, thirty minute, trial violin lesson to help you see if we are the right violin studio for you. If, after the trial violin lesson, you decide not to continue with us, there's absolutely no obligation. Schedule your free trial violin lesson today!
