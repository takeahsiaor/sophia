SOPHIA HSIAO

Owner, Instructor

I graduated in 2009 with a Bachelor of Music degree in Violin Performance from the University of Illinois at Urbana-Champaign. Upon graduation, I pursued missions work with a college ministry called InterVarsity Christian Fellowship where I mentored students who were studying music, art, theater, and film. My passion for students to pursue music began to fuel my desire to become a teacher so that I could equip the next generation of student musicians.

In 2013, I began my private violin studio in Glenview then relocated to Arlington Heights in 2015 where I currently teach in my home. In addition to teaching privately, I have been a violin instructor at the Des Plaines School of Music since 2014.

I have performed in a variety of settings including orchestral and chamber ensembles, weddings and engagement parties, and church events and conferences. My husband and I enjoy playing together as a violin and guitar duo for special events. Some of my favorite activities include spending time with my husband on house projects, trying new restaurants with friends and family, and browsing Pinterest for new craft and recipe ideas.

Every student can learn how to play music when they are given sound instruction and a nurturing environment.

- SOPHIA HSIAO -
