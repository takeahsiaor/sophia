The following is what I ask students and parents to commit to and what I am committed to providing the student and parent.

Teacher commitments

  • Punctual with the lesson times and will come prepared to lessons.
  • Use appropriate exercises and suggestions to improve technique, musicianship, and performance.
  • Continue to pursue growth in teaching skills and knowledge through reading books, online resources, and attending workshops and conferences.
  • Seek to build a relationship with each student and family knowing how crucial that is to the learning process
  • Observe and adapt to the different way each student learns.
  • Honestly communciate with the student (and/or parent if applicable) how the student is progressing in their lessons.

Student commitments

  • Arriving on time and coming prepared to lessons with instrument and books
  • Committed to regular practice at least 4-5 days a week, as it is essential to any musical growth
  • The time required for practice depends heavily on the age and ability of the student and will therefore be discussed with the student on a case by case basis.

Parental commitments (if applicable)

  • For students ages 4-6, parents are welcome to sit in the lesson during the first few weeks. After this time period it is recommended that the parent sit out so that your child can learn independently and start to build a relationship with the teacher.
  • Committed to being involved with home practice and encouraging your child to practice regularly.
  • Responsible for renting or purchasing an instrument for their child so that one is available for lessons and practice

Private Lesson Policy

  • Private lessons are offered weekly.
  • Private lessons will focus on technique, music theory, and musicianship.
  • I will notify the student and/or parent when the student needs to purchase new music books or accessories.

Cancellation Policy

  • Please contact me at least 24 hours prior to the scheduled lesson so that we have time to schedule a make-up lesson.
  • For last-minute cancellations made less than 24 hours before the scheduled lesson, payment is required. Recognizing that emergencies do come up, enforcement of payment is up to the teacher’s discretion.
  • If there is an emergency preventing lesson attendance, please call or text me on my phone.
  • Make-up lessons will be scheduled within 2-4 weeks.
  • Any lessons cancelled due to the teacher’s schedule will be rescheduled as soon as possible and notice will be provided at least 24 hours in advance.

Termination Policy

  • The teacher may terminate lessons due to poor behavior, failure to commit to practice, or failure to comply with the contents of the studio policy
  • If the parent and student wish to terminate lessons, they must communicate this with the teacher two weeks before the desired end date so that appropriate steps can be taken

Recital Policy

  • Recitals are held on a yearly basis.
  • The recital is for students to perform the music they have been working on.
  • Memorization of the pieces is highly encouraged but not required.
  • Family and friends are welcome to attend.
  • Date, time, and location will be communicated with all families at least two months in advance.
